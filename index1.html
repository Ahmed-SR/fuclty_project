PIZZA

It is a food that has its origins in eastern Mediterranean countries such as Greece and Turkey, where they cooked a layer of dough on a hot stone and covered it with different types of ingredients such as meat and vegetables, then the Italian soldiers took it from them to take it to Italy with the addition of tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, then Italian immigrants transported it to America At first, its sale was limited to Italian neighborhoods in major cities in America, and Naples is the center of Italian pizza. It has become known worldwide, and pizza is one of the main meals and can be considered as an appetizer.

How to make Italian pizza dough:
  • Mix the water, salt, sugar, yeast and oil together for 5 minutes, and in another bowl mix the flour with garlic powder, milk powder and baking powder.
  • Add the water and yeast mixture to the flour mixture and knead the ingredients together for a quarter of an hour either by hand or with a kneading machine, until you get a cohesive and soft dough, then sprinkle with a spoonful of olive oil and cover and leave it to ferment for an hour in a warm place.
Pizza success secrets:
  1. You must leave the dough to rise for an hour in a warm place so that you can form the dough and spread it and put the filling.

  2. Check the validity of the yeast you are using to get a doughy consistency

  3. When applying water to the flour while making pizza dough, the water should be warm, not boiling or cold.

  4. Never knead the pizza dough again after it has fermented, just cut it into circles and start making pizza.

  5. You can stuff the edges of the pizza dough just like ready, by placing the cheddar cheese pieces and rolling the edges.
